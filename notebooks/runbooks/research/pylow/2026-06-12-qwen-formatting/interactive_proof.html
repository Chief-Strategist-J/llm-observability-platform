Forward pass activation formula
h = xW₀ + (α/r)xBA
x W₀ [1 × k]
Raw pre-trained forward pass mapping of activations through the frozen base weights matrix.
x B [1 × r]
Funnel down-projection. Activations are compressed down into the bottleneck rank space.
(xB) A [1 × k]
Up-projection returning the bottleneck state back to the target layer dimensionality.
Gradient of loss with respect to Adapter A
∇_A ℒ = ∂ℒ/∂A = (α/r) (xB)ᵀ ∇_h ℒ
(xB)ᵀ [r × 1]
Transpose of the bottleneck activation vector from the forward pass.
∇_h ℒ [1 × k]
Incoming loss gradient flowing back from subsequent model layers.
Result [r × k]
Calculated weight updates perfectly matching the shape of matrix adapter A.
Gradient of loss with respect to Adapter B
∇_B ℒ = ∂ℒ/∂B = (α/r) (∇_h ℒ Aᵀ) xᵀ
∇_h ℒ Aᵀ [1 × r]
Backpropagated error signal projected into the bottleneck rank space.
xᵀ [d × 1]
Transpose of the input activation vector.
Result [d × r]
Gradient updates matching the dimensionality of down-projection adapter B.
